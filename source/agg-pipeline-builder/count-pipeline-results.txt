================================
Count Pipeline Results Documents
================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can view the number of documents outputted by your pipeline with the 
:guilabel:`count results` button.

About this Task 
---------------

When you delete or add a document, the :guilabel:`count results` value on the 
:guilabel:`Aggregations` tab doesn't automatically update to reflect the new 
document.

Before You Begin 
----------------

To count result documents, you must first :ref:`create and run 
<create-agg-pipeline>` your aggregation pipeline.

You cannot count result documents while editing your pipeline.

Steps
-----

.. procedure:: 
   :style: normal

   .. step:: Click the count results button

      After you run your pipeline, click :guilabel:`count results`, appears 
      under the :guilabel:`Run` button. The button will update with the current
      count of resulting documents.

      .. figure:: /images/compass/agg-builder-click-count-results.png
         :alt: Click the count results link

   .. step:: (Optional) Click the refresh button 

      If you edit your pipeline, you must press the :icon-fa5:`repeat` button to 
      update your document count.
